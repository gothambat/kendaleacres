Welcome
to Kendale Acres Church

Welcome, and thank you for visiting us online today. Our goal, through our online ministry, is to supply needed information and resources to our church family and to introduce our community to the ministries here at Kendale Acres Church. Please take a few moments to look around and if you do not find the information you are looking for, feel free to contact us and we will do all we can to help assist you.

We are a ministry serving our Lord and Savior in Sanford, NC. We are a member of the National Association of Free Will Baptist. Together it is our desire and goal to reach the world with the message that Jesus Saves. We are a church where the Bible is believed and preached. Where people are friendly and sincerely glad to have you. A church where you are treated as a person not a number. Let me invite you to worship with us, I believe that you will enjoy your visit.
